Best Medicine Cabinets with Built-in Lighting

Medicine cabinets with built-in lighting are a practical and stylish choice for any bathroom renovation. Not only do they provide ample storage for toiletries and medications, but they also improve visibility and make it easier to find what you need in the dark. Features to Consider

When shopping for a medicine cabinet with built-in lighting, there are several features to consider. Some of the key factors to think about include:

  • Lighting Type - Choose between incandescent, LED, or halogen lighting, each with its own pros and cons.
  • Finish - Select from a variety of finishes to match your bathroom's style and aesthetic.
  • Size - Consider the size of the medicine cabinet to ensure it fits your bathroom's layout and storage needs.
  • Mirrored Doors - Some medicine cabinets feature mirrored doors, which can help create the illusion of a larger space.
  • Adjustable Shelves - Consider a medicine cabinet with adjustable shelves to accommodate different-sized bottles and containers.

Installation Tips

Installing a medicine cabinet with built-in lighting can be a straightforward process, but there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Measure Twice - Measure the space where the medicine cabinet will go to ensure it fits properly.
  • Choose the Right Mounting Hardware - Select the appropriate mounting hardware to ensure the medicine cabinet is secure and level.
  • Follow Manufacturer Instructions - Read and follow the manufacturer's instructions for installation to avoid any potential issues.
